Welcome to Spine

Think of this as the Spine to your application. It uses Backbone for structure, Hapi for server management and routing, LESS as a css preprocessor and Grunt as a task runner

There are many other examples of a starter kit that are also out there. For example https://developers.google.com/web/tools/starter-kit/ is an excellent one to use.

Although the Google web starter kit is an excellent tool it is very complex for someone starting out. Because of that, I made this barebones kit with a few pieces just to get you going with the basics. I left out a few tools that you should add, on purpose, because they were pieces I had trouble learning why/how they worked.
